# Engineering a Sustainable Tech Career

In this session, we will explore practical, reliable, and repeatable practices for helping professionals build successful careers in technology organizations. The guidelines are simple, grounded in decades of mentoring, hundreds of interviews, and real-world career growth experiences.

We will focus on activities across four key areas:

Health & Balance – sustaining mental health, motivation, and confidence
Programming & Algorithms – building strong technical foundations
Communication & Behavior – improving collaboration, influence, and professional relationships
Architecture & Design – developing systems thinking and technical judgment
We will approach software development as a craft, similar to music or yoga. By learning the right skills and practicing consistently—both individually and together—we can achieve meaningful and lasting results.

Join this session to develop practical habits that help you grow your own career and support others in theirs.
